Subfend Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

The short version: Subfend does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Your subscription information stays on your device and is never shared with anyone — including us.

Who We Are

Subfend is developed by TNQ Nexus, an independent app developer. We build privacy-first Android applications.

Data We Collect

None. Subfend is designed to work entirely offline. We do not collect, process, or store any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not require an account or registration
  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not access your bank accounts, financial institutions, or payment methods
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics that identify you
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties

Data Stored on Your Device

All subscription data you enter into Subfend (service names, prices, billing dates, trial end dates, and preferences) is stored locally on your device using an encrypted local database. This data never leaves your phone.

If you uninstall Subfend, all locally stored data is permanently deleted. Premium users can manually export and import their data using the backup feature — this creates a local file on your device that you control entirely.

Crash Reporting

Subfend uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports when the app encounters an error. This helps us identify and fix bugs. Crash reports contain:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version
  • Technical crash information (stack traces)

Crash reports do not contain any of your subscription data, personal information, or financial details. You can opt out of crash reporting in your device's Google settings.

Google Play Purchases

If you choose to purchase Subfend Premium, the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play. We do not have access to your payment information (credit card number, billing address, etc.). Google's privacy policy governs these transactions.

Notifications

Subfend sends local notifications to remind you about upcoming bills and trial end dates. These notifications are generated and delivered entirely on your device — they do not go through our servers or any third-party push notification service. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings or within the app.

Children's Privacy

Subfend is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Since we don't collect your contact information, we recommend checking this page periodically.
